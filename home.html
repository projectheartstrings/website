About Us!

We are Project Heartstrings, a ground-up student initiative seeking to raise funds for a number of organisations supporting health and humanitarian causes for marginalised communities via creative merchandise sales. Through our efforts, we hope to promote the rights of migrant workers, youth facing adversity, people with physical disabilities, and survivors of slavery, trafficking and abuse.

Our Beneficiaries!

Click on the names to learn more about them!


Hagar International

An international organisation dedicated to the recovery of women and children who have endured extreme abuses.


HealthServe

A non-profit organisation dedicated to providing healing and hope to migrant workers.


Impart

A non-profit organisation in Singapore that pioneers volunteer-driven community solutions to enable transformative youth development.


MDAS

To maximise the quality of life of people with Muscular Dystrophy and their families, to integrate them into society and support research towards a cure.


Limitless

A non-profit organization founded with the desire to see every youth given the opportunity to live out their potential.


The Safe Place

To empower women and families with unsupported pregnancies to make life-giving choices.
